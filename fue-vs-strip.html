FUE Procedures vs. Strip Method

Understand the key differences between the two main hair transplant approaches.

Choosing the Right Procedure

Two primary surgical hair restoration methods exist: Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and the Linear Strip Method (FUT). Both can produce outstanding results — the best choice depends on your goals, lifestyle, and the extent of your hair loss.

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction)

  • No Linear Scar
  • Minimal Downtime
  • Can Wear Hair Short
  • Individual Follicle Extraction
  • Ideal for Smaller Sessions

FUE individually extracts hair follicles using a small punch tool — no linear scar, ideal for patients who prefer short hair.

Learn About NeoGraft® FUE

FUT (Linear Strip Method)

  • Maximum Grafts Per Session
  • Shorter Procedure Time
  • Cost-Effective Option
  • Ideal for Advanced Hair Loss
  • Excellent Density

FUT removes a donor strip to maximize grafts in one session — ideal for patients with more advanced or diffuse hair loss.

Learn About FUT Strip Method

The best way to determine the ideal procedure is a one-on-one consultation with one of our specialists. We'll evaluate your donor supply, hair loss pattern, lifestyle, and budget — and give you our honest recommendation.
