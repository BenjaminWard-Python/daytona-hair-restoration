Minimally Invasive

NEOGRAFT®

Your Own Hair. Naturally.

  • Painless
  • Scarless
  • No Incision
  • Natural-Looking Results

A minimally invasive hair replacement method, NeoGraft® has been shown to stimulate the development of thicker hair. The NeoGraft® procedure transfers healthy hair follicles from your scalp to areas of thinning hair to restart the hair growth cycle. NeoGraft® combines the Follicular Unit Extraction method (FUE) with automated technology to eliminate the time disadvantage of the manual FUE method and improve on all the cosmetic disadvantages of the strip method.

Revolutionary Technology

ARTAS®

Robotic Hair Transplant

  • Less Invasive
  • Restores Thick Hair
  • Stimulates Hair Growth
  • Natural-Looking Results

ARTAS® hair transplant is a groundbreaking hair restoration technique that stimulates follicles in areas of thinning hair to grow healthier, stronger hair. This innovative hair transplant surgery uses healthy hair follicles as donors and places them in areas of thinning hair to promote proper hair regrowth — with exceptional precision provided by robotic technology.

SMARTGRAFT®

Innovative Hair Transplant Procedure

  • No Incisions
  • No Linear Scarring
  • More Effective Results
  • Quicker Procedure Times

SmartGraft® is an innovative hair transplant procedure that helps regrow hair for those who are suffering from thinning hair. SmartGraft® is a minimally invasive hair restoration procedure that encourages the growth of healthier hair without the downtime of traditional hair surgeries.

Hair Restoration Option

Linear Strip Method
(FUT)

  • Maximum Graft Count
  • Single Session Coverage
  • Proven Long-Term Results
  • Cost-Effective Option

The Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) strip method is ideal for patients who need the maximum number of grafts transplanted in a single session. A thin strip of donor hair is removed from the back of the scalp, then individual follicular units are carefully dissected and transplanted to the recipient area.

Non-Surgical Solution

Scalp
Micropigmentation

  • Non-Surgical
  • Immediate Results
  • Works for Any Hair Loss Stage
  • Natural-Looking Density

Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP) is a non-surgical cosmetic procedure that uses specialized pigments to replicate the look of natural hair follicles. This treatment creates the appearance of a fuller head of hair or a closely-cropped haircut, effectively camouflaging hair loss at any stage.
