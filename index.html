What We Offer

Our Hair Restoration Treatments

FUE Hair Transplant

Follicular Unit Extraction is our most popular procedure — individually extracting follicles for a natural, scar-free result with minimal downtime.

Learn More

FUT Strip Method

The Follicular Unit Transplantation strip method is ideal for patients needing maximum graft numbers in a single session for denser coverage.

Learn More

Scalp Micropigmentation

A non-surgical cosmetic procedure that replicates the look of hair follicles using specialized pigments — ideal for any stage of hair loss.

Learn More

PRP Therapy

Platelet-Rich Plasma therapy uses your body's own growth factors to stimulate dormant follicles, slow hair loss, and support transplant healing.

Learn More
