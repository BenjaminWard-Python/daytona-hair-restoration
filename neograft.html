NeoGraft® FUE Hair Transplant

Your Own Hair. Naturally.

  • Painless
  • Scarless
  • No Incision
  • Automated Extraction
  • Natural-Looking Results
  • FDA Cleared

NeoGraft® is the first FDA-cleared automated hair transplant system, combining the Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) method with pneumatic technology to harvest follicles quickly, safely, and without a linear scar.

During the procedure, individual hair follicles are extracted from the donor area using a gentle automated punch, then carefully implanted into areas of thinning hair. Because no scalpel or stitches are used, patients heal quickly and can return to normal activities within days.
